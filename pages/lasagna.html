Lasagna

A classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotto Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Italian seasoning

Steps:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles and prepare the meat sauce.
  2. Layer pasta, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  3. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella.
  4. Bake until golden brown and bubbly.
  5. Let it rest before serving.
