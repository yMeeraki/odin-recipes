Pancakes

Soft, fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast, topped with syrup, butter, or fruits.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, and melted butter in a separate bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Heat a pan and cook pancakes until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve with syrup and toppings of choice.
