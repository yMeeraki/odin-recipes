Greek Salad

A refreshing Mediterranean salad with crisp vegetables, feta cheese, and olives, drizzled with olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Oregano

Steps:

  1. Chop cucumbers, tomatoes, and onions.
  2. Add olives and feta cheese.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Sprinkle oregano and toss gently.
  5. Serve fresh.
